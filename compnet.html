CompNet

I am an external research consultant for CompNet, hosted at the Halle Institute for Economic Research (IWH), working on firm dynamics and productivity.

The Micro Data Infrastructure (MDI)

I'm part of a team of economists and data scientists building the Micro Data Infrastructure (MDI) — a European, cross-country, census-style, firm-level dataset designed for research and policy on competitiveness and firm dynamics.

The infrastructure assembles confidential firm-level data across countries in a harmonised way, making it possible to study reallocation, productivity, and investment with comparable micro data at a European scale.
